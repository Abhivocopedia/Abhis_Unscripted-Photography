The Photographer

Hobby turned
into Art.

Abhinandana Bhatta — known in every frame simply as Abhi — sees the world through the lens of quiet observation. His work lives in the space between planned and unplanned, between the shutter click and the breath before it.

From the golden stillness of wildlife corridors to the raw, fleeting emotion of candid human moments — each photograph is a visual diary entry, left unscripted for a reason.
